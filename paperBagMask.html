The process...

This small project consists of a Snapchat filter that tracks basic facial expressions (especially mouth and eyes) and uses different textures to translate them into a paper bag face. The process was fast and fun. I wanted to make a filter for shy people who want to express themselves without having to show their face, and what better option for that than a technology that can customize reality by taking only what is important!
