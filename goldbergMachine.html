The process...

The purpose of this project was to experiment with Unreal Engine’s physics: acceleration, collision, thrusters, and other forces applied to actors. Some other actors work with blueprints, like the “Start” button, hot air balloon and the final explosions, for example.

The project was planned and executed in a linear way. First I sketched the different steps of the RG machine, then I made the assets in Maya and after that I imported them to Unreal Engine and blueprinted the actors. To finalize the level I set the materials and lighting in order to give it an interesting atmosphere.

During the process -and with a lot of testing- I had to go back and modify some assets or even change certain steps, but I am satisfied with the result, considering that it was my first VR project in Unreal.
