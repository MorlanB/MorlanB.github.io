About the App

The app consists of a linear experience containing different scenes. Since the target user is not necessarily familiar with VR, the first levels focus on letting them get more comfortable with it and learning basic interactions (moving around, grabbing objectsâ€¦). Then, they go through a training level where they learn what the possible hazards are at the factory, and how to identify and avoid them. Once the user knows what they have to do and how to do it, they reach the Assessment level, in which they have to make use of what they have learned so far to get their final score in the experience.
